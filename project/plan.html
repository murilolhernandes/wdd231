Cookbook Corner - Site Plan

Site Name: Cookbook Corner

Site Purpose: A recipe website for finding and sharing recipes

Scenarios: Some of the questions the users may ask will be:

  • How can I find a specific recipe?
  • What are the ingredients for a recipe?
  • How can I contribute my own recipe?
  • How can I search for recipes by cuisine?
  • How can I search for recipes by difficulty level?
  • How can I search for recipes by ingredient?

Color Schema: I'm chose a color scheme that will be warm and give a feeling of coziness:

  • Warm and Cozy: coolors.co/f4f1de-e05b37-81b29a-3d3d3d
    • Primary Color: #F4F1DE
    • Secondary Color: #E05B37
    • Accent Color: #81B29A
    • Text Color: #3D3D3D

Typography: Playfair Display and Open Sans

  • Playfair Display for the Headings
  • Open Sans for the Body
